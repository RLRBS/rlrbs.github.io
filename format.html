Tournament Format

Open Qualifier

All eligible teams will be entered into the "open qualifier" stage of RLRBS.

Seeding will be determined by admins/staff using statistics gathered from RL6Mans NA.

The open qualifier will be a double elimination format.

All initial matches in the open qualifier will be best of three; once play has proceeded to Round of 16, matches in winner’s bracket will be best of five (loser’s bracket will remain best of three for the entirety of the Qualifier).

The final 12 teams remaining at the conclusion of the qualifier will qualify to participate in RLRBS league play.

League Play

League play is a round robin with the twelve teams that qualified through the open qualifier; these teams will play one (1) best of five against every other team.

Rankings will be determined by comparing the total number of match wins received by a team during league play. If there is a tie, ranking will be determined by game differential.

If a team disqualifies from a match for any reason, the match will be recorded as a 3-0 win in favor of the opposing team.

Playoffs

The top six (6) ranked teams after the conclusion of league play will compete in the RLRBS playoffs.

Teams will be seeded from first through sixth based on their performance in league play.

The Playoffs will be a single elimination format for the first round, but will become a double elimination bracket from the second round onwards.

The top two (2) ranked teams after the conclusion of league play will receive a first round bye.

At the conclusion of the playoffs, the winning team will receive the entire prize pool.

Tiebreakers

If two or more teams within league play achieve the same number of match wins, resulting in a tie, the tie(s) will be resolved by applying the following tiebreaking mechanisms:

  • Overall game differential
  • Head to head
